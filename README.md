# Privacy Policy for Galilee Bible
**Last updated:** 04-June-2026

Galilee Bible is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

### 1. Data We Collect
* **Account Information:** When you register, we collect your username and security question/answer to provide a personalized, synchronized Sanctuary experience across your devices.
* **Audio Data:** We request permission to use your device's microphone for the "Recite by Heart" feature. This audio data is transmitted securely to our backend server solely for accuracy scoring and is immediately deleted after processing; it is never permanently stored or shared.
* **Preferences & Progress:** We store your "Favorites" and "Studied" lists locally and on our secure server to track your progress through the Word.

### 2. How We Use Data
We use your data exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve the application's features, including user account synchronization and recitation accuracy evaluation. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

### 3. Third-Party Services
We utilize a secure virtual private server (Contabo VPS) to host our backend logic. For specific features like Text-to-Speech (TTS) and Speech-to-Text (STT) processing, your data may be processed securely by AI models. These third-party services are strictly bound to keep your data confidential and are prohibited from selling your information.

### 4. Data Retention & Deletion
We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You have the right to request the total deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. You can initiate this request directly within the app's settings or by contacting us at the email address below.
